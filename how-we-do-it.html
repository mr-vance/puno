SERVICE

Business Service

This is how it flows at the most fundamental level. This is what you give, how you do it, and what you get, and how you get it. It is also how you receive, how you do it, and everything in between.

Farmer

As a farmer, you tell us how much you need, what you need it for, and submit your documentation to us. Our team will reach out to you within 24 working hours to organize a farm visit. After you receive the money, you will be required to send updates to the investors weekly, and will be receiving random farm visits from the Puno team.

Investor

As an investor, you get to choose the farmer you want to invest in (you are more than welcome to ask for more information regarding the farmer, or to go visit the farm before and after investing), and how much you want to give the farmer, the time it would take for the farmer to pay back, will be predicted as well as the Return of Inestment (ROI).
