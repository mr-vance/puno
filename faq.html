What is Puno's mission?
Puno's mission is to empower smallholder farmers across Africa into commercialization by providing them with access to financial resources and connecting them with investors who believe in their potential.
What is crowd investment, and how does it benefit farmers?
Crowdinvestment is a funding model where multiple investors contribute small amounts to support farming projects. Farmers benefit by receiving the necessary funds to expand their operations and achieve better yields.
How does crowd investment work at Puno?
Crowdinvestment is a unique concept at Puno. Instead of giving farmers money, we purchase their agricultural needs, such as seeds, fertilisers, and equipment. Farmers repay us in cash or produce after each harvest, until full repayment. This approach helps farmers access essential resources without taking on debt.
Can farmers choose to repay their loans in produce instead of cash?
Yes, farmers have the flexibility to repay their loans in either cash or produce. This allows them to choose the repayment method that best suits their circumstances.
How does PunoBuddy support farmers during the harvest season?
PunoBuddy is our innovative support system for farmers. It provides guidance, information, and assistance during the harvest season. Farmers can access valuable resources and expert advice to ensure a successful harvest.
When will Puno's insurance product be available?
We are planning to launch our insurance product in February 2024. This insurance will offer farmers additional financial security and peace of mind.
How can investors benefit from Puno's platform?
Investors on the Puno platform have the opportunity to support smallholder farmers and earn financial returns. By participating in crowd investment, investors can help farmers while benefiting from agricultural growth.
What is Puno's role in connecting farmers to markets?
Puno serves as a bridge between farmers and markets. We facilitate access to markets for farmers, helping them sell their produce efficiently and at fair prices.
Is Puno available to farmers outside South Africa?
Currently, Puno primarily focuses on supporting farmers in South Africa. However, we have plans for expansion to benefit farmers in other regions in the future.
How can I become a Puno agent and work with farmers in my community?
To become a Puno agent, you can apply through our website. Agents play a crucial role in connecting farmers with our platform and services.
How can I get involved with Puno as an investor or farmer?
To get started with Puno as an investor or farmer, simply sign up on our platform. Investors can browse and support projects, while farmers can access resources and financial assistance to improve their farming operations.
Is there a minimum investment amount for investors?
Puno allows investors to start with small amounts, making it accessible to a wide range of individuals. There's no minimum investment requirement.
Can I track the progress of the farming projects I've invested in?
Yes, investors can track the progress of their investments through our user-friendly dashboard, which provides real-time updates on project performance.
