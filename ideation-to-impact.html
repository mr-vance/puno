Puno's Journey from Ideation to Impact

At the heart of every endeavour lies a story, a tale woven with determination, passion, and a resolute vision. This is the story of Puno, a journey that began with a deep-rooted connection to farming and a burning desire to make a difference in the lives of smallholder farmers.

Born and raised in a village nestled within the heart of South Africa, I, Faith Mokgalaka, Founder and CEO of Puno, experienced firsthand the struggles and triumphs of farming. My grandparents were farmers, nurturing the land and reaping its rewards. As fate would have it, my path intertwined with the world of agriculture, and I too ventured into farming, starting my own watermelon farm at the age of 20.

Yet, beneath the surface of our lush fields, I recognized the challenges that farmers faced. Limited access to finance, lack of insurance coverage, and the ever-present uncertainty of nature's whims hindered the growth of these unsung heroes. It was in these challenges that I saw an opportunity, a chance to leverage technology and innovation to transform the landscape of smallholder farming.

Puno was conceived from a deep-rooted belief that smallholder farmers hold the key to Africa's food security and economic prosperity. Our journey has been one of identifying these challenges, dissecting them, and piecing together an innovative approach that paves the way for a brighter future. Our platform is not just a solution; it's a bridge that connects the aspirations of farmers with the resources of investors.

“Puno was conceived from a deep-rooted belief that smallholder farmers hold the key to Africa's food security and economic prosperity.”

Puno Investments Platform

The Puno platform represents a win-win scenario. Farmers gain access to short-term loans and pay-as-you-harvest insurance, enabling them to unlock their full potential. Investors, on the other hand, have the opportunity to support these farmers and reap financial rewards while contributing to the growth of local communities.

Today, I am excited to unveil a glimpse of our Minimum Viable Product (MVP) concept. Through Puno, investors will have the power to directly impact the lives of smallholder farmers. Our platform acts as a conduit, allowing investors to become partners in agricultural growth, one harvest at a time.

Join us in this transformative journey. Become a part of the Puno community, where innovation meets impact, and where small investments sow the seeds of change. Together, we can turn the tides of agricultural opportunity, creating a legacy of empowerment for generations to come.

Stay tuned as we reveal more about Puno's exciting developments and opportunities. Your role in this story is waiting - let's redefine the future of smallholder farming, together.
